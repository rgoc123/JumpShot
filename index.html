JUMPSHOT

Welcome to JumpShot!

Press Enter to start or re-start the game!

The goal of the game is to score as many points as you can by moving the ball on to new platforms.

Use the left and right arrows to move the ball, and get 10 points for every platform you land on. You'll level up every 200 points!

But be careful: the platforms get smaller with each level.

Don't let the ball go more than halfway out of bounds or drop to the bottom!
